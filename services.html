The 4-step Process in Detail

01

Discovery Call

You get to tell me about your business, struggles, and goals.

This step is critical: it provides the insight to enhance, showcase, and bring out your brand’s identity.

That way, you can get copy that feels authentic and performs exceptionally. We dive deep into your business, audience, and goals to understand what makes your offer unique.

02

Research & Strategy

This is where everything from our call comes together.

Proper market research, competitor analysis, and a content framework are created.

The goal is simple: write to position your product or service as the clear, go-to choice for your customers. Comprehensive market research and competitor analysis to craft a winning copy strategy.

03

Copy Creation

No filler words. No AI shortcuts.

Everything is written with the intent to sell and make you more money.

It captures attention, builds trust, and keeps your customers hooked. Your copy is written with conversion psychology and tested frameworks that drive results.

04

Refinement & Delivery

You’ll receive your copy, polished and ready to use.

If something needs to be adjusted, whether it’s the selling points, offer outline, or tone we’ll do one final revision.

I make sure everything aligns with your vision and business goals, without endless back-and-forth. Two rounds of revisions included to ensure the copy perfectly aligns with your vision.
